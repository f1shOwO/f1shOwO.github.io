Tteokbokki

Return to main page

Tteokbokki is a common Korean street food.
It is known to contain spicy rice cakes, fish cake, kimchi, and various vegetables.

How do I make this dish?

In order to make this dish, you will need:

Main Ingredients Sauce Ingredients OptionalGarnish Options

Korean Rice Cakes 350g / 12 oz Gochujang
(Korean Chili Paste)		 3 tbsp Roasted Sesame Seeds 1 tsp
Korean Fish Cakes 150g / 5.3 oz Sugar 1 1/2 tbsp Sesame Oil 1 tsp
Korean Soup Stock 2 cups Soy Sauce 1 tbsp Green Onion 1 Stalk
Onion 60g / 2 oz Garlic 1 tsp
Gochugaru
(Korean Chili Flakes)		 1 tsp

How to make Tteokbokki:

  1. Soak the rice cakes in warm water for about 10 minutes. This is done to help make the rice cakes more tender.
    • If you are using fresh rice cakes, feel free to skip this step.
  2. Boil the soup stock over medium heat and disolve the sauce ingredients in it by stirring with a spatula.
  3. Once the stock is boiling, add in the rice cakes, fish cakes and onion.
  4. Boil for 3-5 minutes or until the rice cakes are cooked.
  5. Then simmer on low heat for 2-4 minutes.
    • This helps thicken the sauce and improve the flavor.
  6. Stir in any chosen garnish options before serving. Enjoy!
