Bulgogi

Return to main page

Bulgogi is a common Korean comfort food.
It is known to contain marinated shaved beef and served with white rice.

How do I make this dish?

In order to make this dish, you will need:

Marinade Ingredients Other Ingredients
Ingredient Amount Ingredient Amount
Boneless Rib Eye Steak 1 1/2 lb Vegetable Oil 3 tbsp
Korean Pear 1/2 pear
Soy Sauce 1/4 cups Optional Garnish Items
Brown Sugar 2 tbsp Ingredient Amount
Sesame Oil 2 tbsp Toasted Sesame Seeds 2 tsp
Garlic 3 cloves Green Onion 2 Stalks
Ginger 1 tbsp
Gochujang
(Korean Chili Paste)		 1 tbsp

How to make Bulgogi:

  1. Add together the shaved beef, roughly chopped pear, soy sauce, brown sugar, sesame oil, garlic, ginger, and Gochujang.
  2. Mix together everything in a bag or container and let marinade from 2 hours to overnight.
  3. Once marinaded, heat 1 tbsp of vegetable oil in a pan on medum-high heat.
  4. Working in batches, add a single layer of steak onto the pan, cooking both sides for 2-3 minutes or untill brown.
  5. Once finished, add desired garnish and serve immedietly. Enjoy!
