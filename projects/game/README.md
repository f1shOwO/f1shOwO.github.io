# Come Bach My Love

## Setting

The story takes place in a small town where the player lives with Bach.

## Map

```mermaid
graph TD;
	Home(((Home)))-->Outside;
	Outside-->Street;
	Street-->Neighbor's House;
	Street-->Town Square;
	Town Square-->Fountain;
```

The player starts in their home.
They can explore, but must eventually come Bach.

## Story

It's Valentines Day and the player recieves a letter from the love of their
life, Bach. They must then go out and explore to find them, completing
puzzles and overcoming obsticles. 

## Global Variables

The important variables are Bach and money. 
Money allows you to get a hint from the fountain. 
Bach decides if you can reach the end of the story.
